Research Interests

I'm interested in everything related to robotics, especially the motion planning and the intersection of RL and optimal control. During my PhD I would like to work on robots that are more oriented towards practical applications (e.g., UAVs, autonomous vehicles, bipedal and quadrupedal robots, etc.). Though now much of my research is about bio-inspired robots, I believe that the research approaches and techniques used for different robotic objects have a lot in common.

Zhihai Bi, Qinyan Zhou, Hongbin Fang

This paper presents a new design and prototype of a Worm-Snake-Inspired Metameric robot with multi-modal locomotion capability. Dynamic models and gait control framwork for the robot are established and tested with experiments.
